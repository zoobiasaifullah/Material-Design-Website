The Evolution of Material Design

From bold beginnings in 2014 to the personalized future of Material You—discover how Google's design system has shaped digital experiences worldwide.

2014

Material Design 1.0

The Bold Beginning

Google introduced Material Design at I/O 2014, revolutionizing digital interfaces with principles inspired by paper and ink. The design language emphasized bold colors, deliberate motion, and tactile surfaces.
